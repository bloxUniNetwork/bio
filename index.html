How To Use

Drag the "Connect RoLinked" button to your Bookmark toolbar on your browser (If you do not see your Bookmarks, press Ctrl + Shift + B)




After you have dragged it to your Bookmark toolbar, go to your Roblox profile / home page and click it once while on it.

Why RoLinked?

Bind a Discord role to anything on Roblox — like being in a group, owning a Gamepass, or having played a game. RoLinked can also change Discord nicknames to Roblox usernames, so you can be confident someone is who they say they are. RoLinked will keep your members roles and nickname up-to-date automatically over time, without your users needing to do anything special. RoLinked integrates with the Discord UI, so you can always update a user as-needed with a click of a button. We will never display any ads to you, or to your members. Our revenue comes 100% from server owners who directly support us. We care about your privacy, and will never track you or collect any unnecessary data.
